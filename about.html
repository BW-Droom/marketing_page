Why Droom?

Easier

We help with the ability to search for an employer or and employee faster than you would if you were on another job search engine. Instead of going through files we get some key words and do it for you. No more hastle of "will this be a right fit?" It might not be the perfect fit of the perfect job the first time, but we promise you that our mission is to find you that perfect dream job or dream employee in as little time possible with our search engine.

Faster

Our search engine quickly allows you to swipe right if you find a job or employee that you might like to save it for later or swipe left to a job or employee that you would not be interested in. Profiles appear on how much of a match the person or company is. Our engine is able to to this with the key words that are entered when making a profile. This way instead of navigating through different web pages you can see what's specifically targeted to you. Once an employee and employer have both swiped right for each other it dings that there's a match!

Smarter

With combining both what the employer and and employee are looking for our algorithm will be sure to find the right match for what you're looking for. No longer will you get workers that are just not fit for the job, or get a job that you dread going to every day. Although everyone has to work that doesn't mean you have to hate where you work. It doesn't help either party. Let us help you find that enjoyable work place that will make everyone happy. So get started with us and it is very simple. Just remember to swipe for your check! .
